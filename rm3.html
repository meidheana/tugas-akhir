What is Coex Aquarium in Seoul?

The best way to experience the city's oceans is to visit one of the four aquariums in the metropolitan area. One of them COEX Aquarium in Gangnam district, Seoul, is one of the largest aquariums in South Korea with more than 40,000 creatures from more than 650 species on display. The COEX Aquarium has 90 exhibition tanks grouped into fourteen "discovery zones", including six themed areas. The aquarium is housed inside the COEX mall, which is part of the larger COEX Convention & Exhibition Center. The aquarium opened in 2000. Each exhibit features a dedicated aquarium tank where visitors can see species of fish native to the site. Apart from fish, other local animals are included in the exhibits such as birds, otters and suitable vegetation are also included in each exhibit. The theme areas in order, are: 1. Check-In 2. Rainbow Room 3. Korean Fish Story 4. Korean Garden 5. Fish in Wonderland 6. Amazonia World 7. Ocean Touch Lab 8. Mangrove and Beach 9. Living Reef Gallery 10. Ocean Kingdom 11. Marine Mammal Village 12. Deep Blue Square 13. Deep Blue Sea Tunnel 14. Jelly Fish Park 15. Penguin Playground 16. Gift Shop In the underwater tunnel exhibition, visitors walk in an acrylic tunnel through a tank filled with 2,000 tons of water. This tank includes sharks and turtles swimming around visitors in all directions. The COEX Aquarium also features several small exhibits, such as a focus on entertaining children. One of the exhibits, entitled "Wonderland", contains a collection of "eccentric fish tanks" including shower cubicles, computer monitors, bathtubs and toilets. Some exhibits specifically allow visitors to touch starfish and shells. With the aquarium theme 'Trip to the Ocean', explore amazing water worlds and follow their journey. With this Coex aquarium discount ticket, you can have fun at Coex aquarium with your children, friends or family. You can visit this tour every day from 10:00 to 20:00.
