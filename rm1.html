1. The cost of a vacation to Jeju Island

Travel to South Korea provides starting from Rp. 9 million for adults, and Rp. 8.6 million for children. In general, the price listed on the package for 1 person includes the cost of air tickets and such, lodging or hotels starting from 3 stars, meals and tours according to the program that has been provided, and tourist entrance tickets.

2. No visa

You only need to prepare a passport for a vacation there. It's just that, you can only be in South Korea for 30 days, no more. Well, there are many choices of tourist attractions on Jeju Island, including:

- Southern Jeju Discovery
- Jeju Venice Land
- Jeju ATV
- Western Jeju Discovery
- Eastern Jeju Discovery
- Yongmeori Coast
- Seogwipo Submarine

There is one more unique tour on Jeju Island, namely the Olle Trails or a path that goes around the island. The road consists of 24 routes with the longest route measuring 23.1 kilometers (Km). Why is Olle Trails called a tour? Because you will be presented with a beautiful view of Jeju Island, and of course it can be a spot for taking pictures!
